Bleu Game Awards

Starting in 2020, the Bleu Game Awards were created to nominate and celebrate games that were impactful and meaningful to players, emphasizing not only new games, but also games that are older but finally getting played. The BGAs are basically a response to a game award system that relies on the press and media and perhaps doesn't have the same perspective as the average consumer of games.
