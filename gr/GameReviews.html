Game Reviews Introduction

This is by far not a comprehensive list of games I've played; I've only been keeping track and creating reviews since December 2019. I'm also developing a formula to give more standard ratings between games, so that they can be somewhat subjectively rated. All of this begins once I've finished playing through the game, when I typically write a simple one page review in my Lechuttum. From there, the reviews get proof-read and digitizing and uploaded to the internet here. (There will be a slight delay before the full review becomes available). Any game without a rating has not been completed yet. Coming Soon™ video reviews where I discuss the game and provide my thoughts. A detailed list of platforms can be found here. The score is out of 10 and uses this formula for determining the final score for the games. The final score breakdown is as follows:

Rating Explaination
10 "Outstanding" - As close to perfect as possible. A leader of its genre, the game to beat, the masterpiece
9 "Excellent" - An amazing game, with some minor flaws that aren't too distracting, but enough
8 "Great" - Impressive efforts, albiet with a few flaws or issues that hold them back, amazing otherwise
7 "Exceeds Expectations" - Solid game: fun, has an audience, a good time. However, can have some hard-to-overlook issues
6 "Good" - It's an alright game. Slightly above average, generally innofensive
5 "Average" - The most meh of ratings. This game is neither good nor bad. An excersize in apathy
4 "Below Average" - Lots of promise, but most of it is lost. Not the worst game, just a disapointing one
3 "Abysmal" - No, Joey, this is not good. Something went very wrong. Conceptually very promising, but just not there on the execution.
2 "Bad" - Oof. A disaster. Full of pain and suffering and not even the good kind
1 "Terrible" - This game just didn't go off. Just too much went wrong. Too many issues and problems to have fun
0 "Falure" - Lowest of the low. No potential, no fun, no ability to salvage in this state.
