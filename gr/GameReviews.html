Game Reviews Introduction

This is by far not a comprehensive list of games I've played; I've only been keeping track and creating reviews since December 2019. I'm also developing a formula to give more standard ratings between games, so that they can be somewhat subjectively rated. All of this begins once I've finished playing through the game, when I typically write a simple one page review in my Lechuttum. From there, the reviews get proof-read and digitizing and uploaded to the internet here. (There will be a slight delay before the full review becomes availabel).
