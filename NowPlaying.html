Now Playing

There are a couple of current games currently being played, here is the list (as well as some long term games that I play off stream). I also keep track of the games I've beaten, review them, then post the reviews here. A link to the game reviews is below.

Now Streaming:

We Happy Few

Having just finished The Outer Worlds, I felt like continuing with another RPG. We Happy Few is a fun game, with a very dark and depressing backstory/history and I'm excited to continue exploring the game to learn exactly what it is that has all the Wells habitents taking Joy. Also, what happened to Pru and who is Mr. Kite???

Hades

I just started playing Hades for the first time on 2/19/21. The game is amazing. I've watched some people play bits just to see if I would like the game before I bought it, but none of that prepared me for how much fun the game is when I'm the one playing it.

7 Days to Die

7 Days is a survival game I recently started playing again thanks to watching a few streamers play it. It's a game I enjoy playing to relax and chill. I especially love the creativity I'm allowed in the game and the fact that structural integrity and gravity actually matter. I will play some 7 Days off stream, just becuase I do like relaxing with some building, but I try to at least stream the bloodmoons, if not some of the building and general looting that occurs.

FIFA

I've enjoyed the FIFA games for a while, I actually have a few things I do with each one, each year. I build myself as a pro and work to become on the Chelsea first team, I manage Chelsea and take them to the top, typically using the current lineup and buying and selling players as needed after the first year, and, as a bit of a challenge to myself, this year I picked Colchester from the 4th tier of English Football and am working to bring them to the top of the world. I will play some matches off stream, just becuase I still have quite a few more games to play before becoming the world champions I intend to be.

Off Stream:

Destiny 2

I have played Destiny since launch and I love the feel of the game. I often use Destiny to unwind as I get ready for bed. I enjoy playing both solo and with friends and frequently use Destiny as a chance to catch up with my friends and just relax and chill.
