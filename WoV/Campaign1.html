Campaign 1

The YouTube Playlist of Campaign 1

Overview:

Though short, Campaign 1 was a first attempt at many things. It was Bleu's first run at being DM and all of his players were new players to the TTRPG world. It was a first attempt at streaming the campaign on Twitch. The campaign begins in the City of Eriath, a small city on the coast of the continent. The company goes on adventures and finds the beginings of a mystery...

Characters:

Primbesar Sularys (Barbarian):

Sularys is a Mercury Dragonborn. Raised by a tribe, Sularys was blessed to be able to raise the ritual hammer, Jhank Guth'orn. All promise of future leadership disapeared when the tribe was ambushed, leaving Sularys the only survivor. Alone and barely alive, she made her way to Eriath where she joined an adventuring group, hoping that she would one day discover the identity of those that attacked her clan.

Fraeya Selevarün (Sorcerer/Rogue):

Fraeya is an Elven princess, though she hid her royal lineage outside her kingdom. When she was young, her mother was murdered; all investigations quickly led to dead ends and more questions than answers. Leveraging her position and connections, Fraeya began her own investigation when she was considered an adult. Her path led her to Eriath, where she met with an adventuring group that she decided to join to further her investigation.

Moxie (Bard/Druid):

Moxie is a Tiefling. After a few hard lessons while growing up, she found an outlet in music and decided to leave her village for the escape of the big city. Her motivations are her own, though she slowly found herself opening up to the animals who sought her out for friendship.

Mara Astorio (Sorcerer):

Mara is a Human who managed to get herself all but expelled from the Towers of Magic. She decided that her talents could still be of use to the right people, and ran into a group of adventurers in Eriath trying to save a child. Knowing that even though secrecy may be her best choice for survival, she couldn't leave a child to their fate. She joined the group of adventurers and began helping them in their quests.

Elios (Ranger):

Elios is an Elf. He was once a happy young Elven child until he got separated from his parents one day, leading to his capture and enslavement by goblins. He then spent much of his childhood serving goblins, until he was saved by a lone woodsman who decided to take the now young adult under his wing and train him to defend and save others. The woodsman introduced Elios to the individuals he would adventure with.

Thorin Runebreaker (Wizard):

Thorin is a Dwarf who tired of his sheltered library life. When a group of companions was able to banish a white dragon, he knew he had to join them. With his eagle companion, he joined the group of adventurers right at the end of their adventures.

Vicar Bronson (Fighter):

Vicar is a Half-Orc. Bastard son of a great lord (sort of), he was raised on chivalry. However, when his parents had a second child, he found himself cast to the wayside and the target of an assassination plot. He immediately set out for unknown shores, eventually making his way to Eriath. He joined a group of adventurers and ended up falling in love with an elf. After months of trying, he finally found a way to her heart. He then left the group to assist and travel with her.

Alura (Rogue):

Alura is an elf who was deep in the adventures. Unfortunately, some aspects of her background came back to haunt her as she was kidnapped while exploring the sewers underneath Eriath. Her disappearance remains a mystery to this day.

Meztli Sadri (Cleric):

Meztli is a human cleric of Selune who, after begining the adventure, was called away by the Temple of Selune in Eriath.
