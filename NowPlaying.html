Now Playing

There are a couple of current games currently being played, here is the list (as well as some long term games that I play off stream). I also keep track of the games I've beaten, review them, then post the reviews here. A link to the game reviews is below.

Now Streaming:

Hades

I just started playing Hades for the first time on 2/19/21. The game is amazing. I've watched some people play bits just to see if I would like the game before I bought it, but none of that prepared me for how much fun the game is when I'm the one playing it.

Pillars of Eternity

I started playing Pillars last year and made some good progress before I got sidetracked by other games. That being said, I'd really like to finish Pillars and play the sequel as well! I'm having quite a bit of fun not only in the story but also finding all of the voice actors who I can recognize in the game. The worldbuilding and lore in Pillars is astounding and very deep, something else I love in the games I play.

7 Days to Die

7 Days is a survival game I recently started playing again thanks to watching a few streamers play it. It's a game I enjoy playing to relax and chill. I especially love the creativity I'm allowed in the game and the fact that structural integrity and gravity actually matter.

FIFA

I've enjoyed the FIFA games for a while, I actually have a few things I do with each one, each year. I build myself as a pro and work to become on the Chelsea first team, I manage Chelsea and take them to the top, typically using the current lineup and buying and selling players as needed after the first year, and, as a bit of a challenge to myself, this year I picked Colchester from the 4th tier of English Football and am working to bring them to the top of the world.

Off Stream:

Destiny 2

I have played Destiny since launch and I love the feel of the game. I often use Destiny to unwind as I get ready for bed. I enjoy playing both solo and with friends and frequently use Destiny as a chance to catch up with my friends and just relax and chill.
