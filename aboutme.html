About Me

Hello there! My name is CodeBleu13, but please, call me Blue (or Bleu). I'm at the end of my 20s, use he/him pronouns, and enjoy beer (not IPAs), gin, and scotch. I listen to music, and occasionally (always) revert back to my high school tastes in punk, rock, and oldies. And some folk music (like Mongolian Throat Singing or viking/old germanic inspired music). I enjoy playing games, especially RPGs, as well as the occasional strategy game. Oh, or survival game. Or simulation game. OR, or, open world game. Ok, so I'll play pretty much anything at least once. I typically enjoy streaming my first time playthroughs of games where I go in (mostly) without knowing much of anything about the game. I've found that I especially love games that involve making really tough decisions that have consequences down the line and agonizing over whether or not I've done the right thing. I also enjoy D&D and fantasy worlds and even created a homebrew D&D world in which I recently ran my first campaign as DM (Dungeon Master!) which actually just came to an end early in 2021. I live in Pennslyvania where, in a few months, I'll be marrying my best friend (super excited, but wedding planning is abysmal). I love pineapples, puns, and quesooooo (say it with a long drawn out 'o' sound). I've also been known to write the occasional parody song and then attempt to sing (very poorly) that song. I enjoy anime and am always open to suggestions for new ones to watch.

Outside streaming, I enjoyed (pre-plaguetimes) hanging out with friends and going out to places. I am a fishkeeper who has recently rediscovered their love of fishkeeping. I have 2 betta fish, named Glorfindel and Tai Li, that I care for (in separate 10 gallon tanks), as well as their tank mates (neon tetras and ghost shrimp). I enjoy soccer (aka football) and follow a quite Blue team from London. In 2018, after working for 8 years in an Emergency Department of a Inner-city Level 1 Trauma Center, I decided to return to school, not for medicine, as I originally intended, but instead for Computer Science. In December of 2020, I graduated with my second Bachlors Degree. I actually found a huge love for data, and even have a special project that involves gaming and data that I love.

In my free time, I also enjoy modular origami, Something I forgot, but no worries, problem for Future Bleu.
